# AutoMSP UAE/GCC Client Interaction Guide

## Overview

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the process for interacting with clients in the UAE/GCC region, ensuring consistent and successful engagements. AutoMSP specializes in providing AI automation solutions to MSPs and enterprises, including those in the UAE and GCC.

## Step-by-step process

1.  **Initial Contact:** Reach out to potential clients via email or LinkedIn.
2.  **Needs Assessment:** Schedule a discovery call to understand client needs.
3.  **Solution Presentation:** Present AutoMSP solutions tailored to their requirements.
4.  **Proposal & Agreement:** Provide a detailed proposal and finalize the agreement.
5.  **Onboarding:** Onboard client onto AutoMSP platform.
6.  **Ongoing Support:** Provide continuous support and maintenance.

## Scripts/templates where applicable

Best Regards,
[Your Name]

*   **Discovery Call Questions:**

    *   What are your business goals?
    *   Which areas are you looking to automate?
