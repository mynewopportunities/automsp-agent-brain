# Client Communication Templates - AutoMSP

This document provides Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and templates for client communication at AutoMSP. Each SOP outlines the overview, step-by-step process, scripts/templates, tools used, and success metrics for a specific communication scenario.

## SOP 1: Onboarding New Clients

**Overview:**
This SOP details the process of welcoming and onboarding new clients to AutoMSP, ensuring a smooth and positive initial experience.

**Step-by-step process:**
1.  **Welcome Email:** Send a personalized welcome email within 24 hours of contract signing.
2.  **Introductory Call:** Schedule a 30-minute introductory call to understand the client's specific needs and goals.
3.  **Account Setup:** Create client accounts on relevant platforms and share access information.
4.  **Training Resources:** Provide access to training materials and documentation.
5.  **Check-in Call:** Schedule a follow-up call within one week to address any questions or concerns.

**Scripts/Templates:**

*   **Welcome Email:**

    ```
    Subject: Welcome to AutoMSP!

    Dear [Client Name],

    Welcome to AutoMSP! We're thrilled to have you as a partner.

    This email confirms your engagement with AutoMSP for AI automation services. We are confident we'll dramatically increase efficiency for your MSP.

    Next steps:
    1.  We would like to schedule a 30-minute introductory call to learn about your specific needs and goals. Please let us know what time works.
    2.  We have attached a brief questionnaire which will help us understand your current infrastructure and processes.

    We are excited to begin this journey with you!

    Sincerely,
    [Your Name]
    AutoMSP
    ```

**Tools Used:**
*   Email client (e.g., Gmail, Outlook)
*   Calendar scheduling tool (e.g., Calendly)
*   AutoMSP client portal

**Success Metrics:**
*   Positive client feedback on onboarding experience
*   Client satisfaction score (CSAT) of 4.5 or higher
*   Completion of onboarding tasks within one week

## SOP 2: Responding to Support Requests

**Overview:**
This SOP outlines the process for efficiently and effectively responding to client support requests.

**Step-by-step process:**
1.  **Acknowledge Receipt:** Acknowledge the support request within one hour.
2.  **Investigate Issue:** Thoroughly investigate the reported issue.
3.  **Provide Solution/Update:** Provide a solution or an update on the investigation within 4 hours.
4.  **Follow-up:** Follow up with the client to ensure the issue is resolved to their satisfaction.
5.  **Document Solution:** Document the solution in the knowledge base.

**Scripts/Templates:**

*   **Acknowledgement Email:**

    ```
    Subject: Re: [Support Request Subject]

    Dear [Client Name],

    Thank you for reaching out to AutoMSP support.

    We have received your support request and are currently investigating the issue. We will provide an update within 4 hours.

    Sincerely,
    AutoMSP Support Team
    ```

**Tools Used:**
*   Ticketing system (e.g., Zendesk, Freshdesk)
*   Knowledge base (e.g., Confluence, Notion)
*   Remote access tools (e.g., TeamViewer, AnyDesk)

**Success Metrics:**
*   First response time under one hour
*   Resolution time under 24 hours
*   Client satisfaction score (CSAT) of 4.0 or higher
*   Reduction in support ticket volume

## SOP 3: Proactive Status Updates

**Overview:**
This SOP ensures regular and transparent communication with clients regarding the status of their AI automation implementations.

**Step-by-step process:**
1.  **Schedule Updates:** Schedule weekly or bi-weekly status update calls.
2.  **Prepare Agenda:** Prepare an agenda outlining key progress points, challenges, and upcoming milestones.
3.  **Conduct Call:** Conduct the status update call, providing a clear and concise overview of the project's progress.
4.  **Document Outcomes:** Document the key outcomes and action items from the call.
5.  **Follow-up:** Follow up on action items and address any client concerns.

**Scripts/Templates:**

*   **Status Update Email Template:**

    ```
    Subject: AutoMSP - Weekly Status Update - [Client Name]

    Dear [Client Name],

    This email provides a summary of the status of your AI automation implementation with AutoMSP.

    Key progress points this week:
    *   [List key accomplishments]

    Challenges encountered:
    *   [List any challenges and solutions]

    Upcoming milestones:
    *   [List upcoming milestones]

    We would like to schedule a brief call to discuss this update in more detail. Please let me know what time works for you.

    Sincerely,
    [Your Name]
    AutoMSP
    ```

**Tools Used:**
*   Project management software (e.g., Asana, Trello)
*   Calendar scheduling tool (e.g., Calendly)
*   Video conferencing platform (e.g., Zoom, Google Meet)

**Success Metrics:**
*   Adherence to schedule status update calls
*   Positive client feedback on transparency and communication
*   Reduced client escalations due to proactive communication
