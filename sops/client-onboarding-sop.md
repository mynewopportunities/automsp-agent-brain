# Client Onboarding SOP - AutoMSP

## Overview
This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) details the steps for effectively onboarding new clients at AutoMSP, ensuring a smooth transition from sales to implementation. Our focus is on North American MSPs and UAE/GCC enterprises seeking AI automation solutions.

## Step-by-step process
### 1. Pre-Onboarding & Welcome (Day 1)
*   **Account Setup:** Create new client record in CRM and project management system (e.g., Asana, Trello).
*   **Internal Handoff:** Sales team provides comprehensive client brief to the project manager and technical lead, including contract details, identified pain points, and agreed-upon scope.
*   **Welcome Kit:** Send personalized welcome email to the client, including:
    *   Introduction to their dedicated project manager and key team members.
    *   Overview of the onboarding process roadmap.
    *   Link to client portal/communication channels.
    *   Initial information request (e.g., access credentials for relevant systems - securely via encrypted portal only).

### 2. Discovery & Planning (Week 1)
*   **Kick-off Meeting (Virtual):** Conduct a comprehensive kick-off call with client stakeholders to:
    *   Re-introduce team members (AutoMSP & Client).
    *   Review project scope, objectives, and success metrics.
    *   Establish primary communication channels and frequency.
    *   Define key milestones and timelines.
    *   **Actionable Insight (PROOF: Pre-call nurture is critical; strategies to reduce ghosting):** Reiterate value proposition and reinforce how AutoMSP will solve their specific challenges to build immediate buy-in and prevent early disengagement.
*   **Detailed Needs Assessment:** Gather granular information on existing infrastructure, data sources, specific automation processes, and integration points.
*   **Solution Design & Proposal:** Develop a detailed solution design, outlining specific AI automation models, integration points, and expected outcomes. Present to client for approval.

### 3. Implementation & Integration (Weeks 2-X)
*   **Development & Configuration:** AutoMSP technical team develops and configures AI automation solutions according to the approved design.
*   **Regular Updates:** Provide clients with weekly progress reports and conduct bi-weekly sync calls.
*   **Client Review & Feedback:** Incorporate client feedback at key development stages.

### 4. Training & Handover (Final Week of Implementation)
*   **User Training:** Conduct comprehensive training for client staff on using and managing the new AI automation systems.
*   **Documentation:** Provide detailed user manuals and technical documentation.
*   **Final Review & Acceptance:** Obtain formal client sign-off on project completion.

### 5. Post-Onboarding & Support (Ongoing)
*   **Transition to Support:** Seamless handover to AutoMSP's ongoing support team.
*   **Regular Check-ins:** Project manager schedules quarterly business reviews to assess impact and identify new opportunities.

## Tools Used
*   **CRM (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot):** Client record management.
*   **Project Management Software (e.g., Asana, Trello):** Task tracking, milestone management.
*   **Secure File Sharing/Client Portal:** For sensitive information exchange.
*   **Communication Platform (e.g., Slack, Microsoft Teams):** For daily communication.
*   **Video Conferencing (e.g., Zoom, Google Meet):** For meetings.

## Success Metrics
*   **Client Satisfaction Score (CSAT):** Measured via post-onboarding surveys.
*   **Time-to-Value:** Duration from contract signing to initial solution deployment and tangible client benefit.
*   **Onboarding Completion Rate:** Percentage of clients completing all onboarding steps.
*   **Support Ticket Volume (post-onboarding):** Low volume indicates effective training and stable deployment.
