# Discovery Call Script - AutoMSP

## Overview
This script outlines the structured approach for conducting discovery calls with potential clients, primarily North American MSPs and UAE/GCC enterprises. The goal is to deeply understand their current AI automation needs, identify pain points, and qualify them for AutoMSP's solutions.

## Step-by-step process
### 1. Introduction (2 minutes)
*   Welcome and thank you for their time.
*   Briefly state AutoMSP's value proposition: "AutoMSP helps North American MSPs and UAE/GCC enterprises streamline operations and enhance efficiency through AI automation."
*   Set expectations for the call: 15-20 minutes, focusing on understanding their challenges, and determining if there's a good fit.

### 2. Qualification & Needs Analysis (10-12 minutes)
*   **Open-ended questions:**
    *   "What are your primary business goals for the next 6-12 months?"
    *   "Could you describe your current automation processes? What's working well, and what are the biggest challenges?"
    *   "Where do you see the most significant bottlenecks or inefficiencies in your operations?"
    *   "What AI initiatives, if any, have you explored or implemented in the past? What were the outcomes?"
*   **Specific probes based on agent intel (PROOF: Pre-call nurture is critical; strategies to reduce ghosting):**
    *   "How do you typically prepare for new technology implementations? What resources do you typically use?"
    *   "What are your key concerns or doubts when considering AI automation?"

### 3. AutoMSP Value Alignment (5 minutes)
*   Summarize their pain points to confirm understanding.
*   Briefly explain how AutoMSP's AI automation solutions address their specific challenges.
*   "Based on what you've shared about [pain point 1] and [pain point 2], our [relevant AutoMSP solution] could potentially help you [achieve specific benefit]."

### 4. Next Steps & Closing (1-2 minutes)
*   Suggest a deeper dive or a demo call.
    *   "Would you be open to a follow-up call to explore how our solutions could be tailored to your specific environment and demonstrate some practical applications?"
*   Confirm availability and send calendar invite.
*   Thank them for their time.

## Scripts/Templates
### Pre-Call Nurture DM Template (Internal)
This is NOT sent during the call itself, but is a pre-call content strategy to improve show rates and engagement:

```
Hi [Prospect Name],

Excited for our call on [Date] at [Time]! To make the most of our time, I've put together a brief overview of how AutoMSP helps [their industry/role] streamline [common pain point] with AI automation. [Link to relevant, concise content, e.g., a short case study or infographic].

Looking forward to understanding your unique challenges and opportunities!
[Your Name] - AutoMSP
```

*   **Video Conferencing Tool (e.g., Zoom, Google Meet):** For conducting calls.

## Success Metrics
*   **Discovery Call to Demo Conversion Rate:** Percentage of discovery calls that lead to a follow-up demo.
*   **Qualification Rate:** Percentage of discovery calls where the prospect is deemed a good fit.
*   **No-Show Rate:** Minimize the percentage of scheduled calls that are missed (influenced by pre-call nurture).
*   **Average Call Duration:** Keep calls within the allocated time while achieving objectives.
