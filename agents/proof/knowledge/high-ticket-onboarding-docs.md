### 5. Onboarding Kit (Welcome Email)
The exact welcome email to send after contract signing:

---
**Subject:** Welcome to [Your Company] — Your Onboarding is Complete!

Hey [Name],

I'm thrilled to inform you that your onboarding process as a valued client is now complete!

It's a pleasure to welcome you officially as my [service type] partner. I deeply appreciate your trust and look forward to [delivering service] to help [company name] flourish.

I have attached a small 'Welcome Kit' with 3 key elements:

**1. Project Tracker** — A tracker for all project deliverables to stay aligned and track planned work and feedback. [Add link to project tracker/folder]

**2. Communication Plan** — How and when we will communicate for optimal coordination. Also, please join the Slack channel (if active Slack user). If not, LinkedIn is fine. [Add doc with communication policies & timeline]

**3. Content/Project Brief** — Please fill it out within 5-7 days so I can understand your business objectives better. [Add Google Form link]

If you have any laid down policies, style guides, or format structures, please share them with me.

Let me assure you that I aim to exceed your expectations, and YOUR satisfaction is my top priority.

Regards,
[Your Name]

---

## AutoMSP Adaptation

### For MSP AI Automation Clients:
1. **Invoice** — Monthly retainer + one-time setup fee (use AED for UAE, USD for North America)
2. **Contract** — AI automation service agreement with SLA, uptime guarantees, data privacy clauses
3. **SOW** — Specific automation workflows to be built (n8n, ServiceNow, LiveKit voice agent)
4. **Intake Form** — Collect: current tech stack, pain points, team size, budget, timeline
5. **Welcome Email** — Send with:
   - Project Tracker (Notion board link)
   - Communication Plan (Slack channel + weekly check-in schedule)
   - Technical Brief (collect API credentials, system access, workflow requirements)

### Key Principle
> "By onboarding your clients professionally, you win their trust, show your professionalism, and ensure a smooth workflow."

### When to Use
- After client signs contract and pays first invoice
- Before starting any technical work
- Sets expectations and establishes professional relationship from day one
