## What is an AI Agent Architect?
Someone who designs, builds, and deploys multi-agent AI systems that run business workflows autonomously — across sales, ops, finance, and HR — without requiring a CS degree.

## 12-Week Roadmap

### MONTH 1 — Foundations
| Week | Topic | Outcome | Tools |
|------|-------|---------|-------|
| 1 | AI Landscape & Python Basics | Write first 10-line script | Python 3.11, VS Code, Google Colab |
