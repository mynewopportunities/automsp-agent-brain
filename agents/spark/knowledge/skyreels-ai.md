## Skyreels AI

**What it is:** An AI-powered Image to Video (I2V) generator that transforms static images into animated videos with realistic facial expressions and natural movements. It also has a Text to Video mode.

**Key Features:**
*   **Hollywood-Level Facial Animation:** Captures 33 distinct facial expressions and 400+ natural movement combinations.
*   **Dual-Mode Generation:** Supports both Text to Video AI and Image to Video AI modes.
*   **Cinematic Lighting System:** Produces professional lighting effects and scene compositions.
*   **Multi-GPU Acceleration:** Supports parallel processing across 8+ GPUs for faster rendering.
*   **Character Consistency:** Maintains character consistency in both Text to Video AI and Image to Video modes.
* Offers 720P/1080P outputs with cinematic lighting and composition.
* Outputs MP4 videos at 24-60 FPS. Text to Video AI supports 97-289 frame sequences, while I2V mode offers 544x960px to 720P resolutions.

**Pricing:**
*   Free tier: 3 video generations/month.
*   Pro tier: Unlimited Text to Video AI and I2V generations.

**How AutoMSP can use it for clients:**
*   **Advertising:** Create engaging video ads from product photos or marketing materials.
*   **Social Media Content:** Transform static images into animated content for increased engagement.
*   **Virtual Tours:** Animate property photos to create virtual tours automatically.
*   **Educational Content:** Animate diagrams and illustrations to explain complex concepts.

**Relevant Use Cases:**
*   Animating product photos for e-commerce.
*   Bringing portrait galleries to life.
*   Creating virtual property tours.
*   Animating diagrams for educational purposes.
*   Generating animated social media content.
* Generating short films and advertising content.
